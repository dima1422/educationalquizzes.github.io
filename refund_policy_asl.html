Refund & Subscription Policy

asleasy.com

Last updated: May 2025

  • No automatic refunds are provided for subscription payments after the initial 7-day period from the date of purchase.
  • Within 7 days of purchase, you may request a full refund if you are not satisfied. Refunds will be processed within 5–10 business days to the original payment method.
  • After 7 days, subscriptions are non-refundable. However, you can cancel your subscription at any time to prevent future billing.
  • All cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period. You will continue to have access until then.
  • Refunds are not issued for partial months or for accounts suspended/terminated due to violation of our Terms of Service.

How to request a refund or cancel:

Email us at support@asleasy.com with your account email and reason for the request.

Note: We reserve the right to refuse refund requests that appear to be fraudulent or made in bad faith.
